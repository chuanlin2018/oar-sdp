The NIST PDL and associated data are stored in NIST’s public data repository. Please refer to the NIST Open Data website for additional organizational policy and related NIST directive information.
In accordance with U.S. Government Open Data Policy and the NIST Plan for Providing Public Access to the Results of Federally Funded Research, NIST maintains a publicly accessible inventory of available data, the NIST Public Data Listing (PDL). This online inventory is harvested by the Department of Commerce and made publicly available at Data.gov. These NIST PDL complies with the guidance set forth in OMB M-13-13 policy and uses the Project Open Data standard metadata schema representation.

NIST’s public data repository records are accessible in machine-readable open data formats and demonstrate capabilities as set forth in new public law, Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act (115-435) Title II, Open Government Data Act (2019).

As part of NIST strategic efforts to develop infrastructure supporting public access and discoverability to government research datasets, this page will be updated to reflect changes in content and services as they pertain to policy and law.
