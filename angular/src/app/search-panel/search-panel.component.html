Basic Syntax Rules
Search Criteria Keyword Examples Description
Individual terms NIST Chemistry Records that include either “NIST” OR “Chemistry” are returned.
Phrase "NIST Chemistry" Records that contain the phrase “NIST Chemistry” (the two words adjacent to each other in that order) will be returned. 
Phrase with Individual terms "NIST Chemistry" Physics Search Results will match the Phrase “NIST Chemistry” OR “Physics”; That is, returned records will contain the phrase “NIST Chemistry” or “Physics”.
Key  Value pair contactPoint.fn=Linstrom Records whose field, contactPoint.fn, contains “Peter Linstrom” are returned.
Logical Operation "NIST Chemistry" AND Physics Search Results will match the Phrase “NIST Chemistry” AND “Physics”; That is, returned records will contain both the phrase “NIST Chemistry” and “Physics”. Available operators are AND, OR and NOT (all caps).
To search words "AND", "OR" or "NOT", put them in quotes.
